Course Description. This class blends lifestyle design with accessible luxury. We’ll study café culture, the Carrie Bradshaw writing mindset, travel planning (hello, Amalfi Coast), personal finance for big dreams, and everyday rituals that make life feel cinematic — all through an inclusive, realistic lens.

Learning Outcomes

  • Curate café experiences using accessibility‑friendly checklists (noise, seating, menus, Wi‑Fi, outlets).
  • Develop a personal “Carrie‑core” writing ritual and publish two polished lifestyle essays.
  • Plan an inclusive, budget‑aware luxury trip to the Amalfi Coast (or your dream city), with alt options.
  • Build a moodboard & mini‑brand kit (colors, fonts, textures) that reflects your authentic aesthetic.
  • Create a 30‑day micro‑ritual plan that elevates daily life without overspending.

Key Policies

Accessibility & Inclusion

We design for everyone. Request accommodations anytime; materials are provided in accessible formats. Images include alt text; videos include captions. If you spot a barrier, tell me and we’ll fix it fast.

Community Guidelines

  • Be kind, curious, and credit sources and creators.
  • No gatekeeping — luxury is a feeling we can all access.
  • Feedback = specific, actionable, respectful.

Academic Integrity

Share inspiration, not plagiarism. Cite outfits, images, and itineraries you reference.

Resources

  • Budget Tools: Simple spreadsheet + sinking funds template (provided in LMS).
  • Café Map: Local list with noise level, step‑free access, and outlets annotated.
  • Travel Access: Links to accessible stays and transit tips for the Amalfi Coast.
Safety Note. Field work is daylight‑first. Share location with a buddy, hydrate, and mind your budget.
